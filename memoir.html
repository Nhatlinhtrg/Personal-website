STEVEN IS AN ACCOMPLISHED INVESTOR IN THE HEALTH AND WELLNESS SPACE.

With notable investments including Huel - UK's fastest growing e-commerce company internationally, and Zone - the personalised nutrition programme created by the world's top scientists. Previous investments include The Healing Company, edu Lab Sciences, Alto Neuroscience and Abcdelcom. Steven's other investments focus on blockchain technologies, biotech, space, Web3 and consumer technology.

Steven co-founded Flightstory - a media, marketing and investment company consisting of Flight Fund, Flight Ventures, Flightabug, Flight Studio, Flight Talent and Flight Tech.

Steven co-founded thirdweb - a software platform, backed by Shopify and Coinbase, that makes it easy to build web3 applications.

Multiple bestselling author, his recent book 'The Diary Of A CEO: The 33 Laws for Business & Life' was a Sunday Times Number 1 bestseller and has been translated into 35 languages, becoming the fastest selling book of all time in its market and within its category.
